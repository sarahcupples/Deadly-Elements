Plutonium

PRONOUNCIATION: PLOO - TONE - EE - UM

All elements with an atomic number of 84 and above are radioactive, making Plutonium is the deadliest of all the naturally occurring elements with an atomic number of 94. It's also very toxic, in fact about 500g of plutonium dust released into the air would be enough to kill nearly 2 million people. This makes it perfect for nuclear weapons.
