Francium

PRONOUNCIATION: FRAN - SEE - UM

Francium is an extremely reactive element. It's so deadly and explosive that scientists aren't allowed to use it outside of research, and only a tiny amount is created at a time. In fact, the amount created by mankind isn't even enough to weigh!
